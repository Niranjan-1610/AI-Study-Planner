# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and community members, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or socioeconomic status.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Be Respectful**: Treat other community members with respect and dignity
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome and support people of all backgrounds and identities
- **Be Constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and focus on the code, not the person
- **Be Patient**: Remember that people are doing this in their free time
- **Be Collaborative**: Work together to resolve conflicts and improve the project
- **Use Inclusive Language**: Avoid language that could be discriminatory or offensive

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or threats of any kind
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics
- Deliberate intimidation or stalking
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate and fair corrective action
- Removing, editing, or rejecting contributions that violate this code
- Banning temporarily or permanently anyone engaged in behavior they deem inappropriate

## Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. **Direct Message**: Contact a project maintainer
2. **Email**: Send details to conduct@example.com
3. **GitHub Issue**: Create a confidential issue (if available)

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. We are committed to:

- Keeping the reporter's identity confidential (when possible)
- Responding within 48 hours
- Taking appropriate action
- Following up with the outcome

## Enforcement

Consequences for unacceptable behavior may include:

1. **Warning**: Official warning about the behavior
2. **Temporary Ban**: Temporary suspension from community interactions
3. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from the project community

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.

## Questions?

If you have questions about this Code of Conduct, please:

- **Email**: conduct@example.com
- **Open an Issue**: Use the issue tracker (private/confidential if possible)
- **Message a Maintainer**: Direct contact with leadership
